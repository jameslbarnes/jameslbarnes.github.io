The Convent is a nonprofit community in a 28,000 sq ft historic building in Greenpoint, Brooklyn — a residency, workspace, and events institution at the intersection of frontier art and technology.

We're a small group of collaborators evaluating our first 100 founding stewards: people who shape the community both financially and energetically. Workshops, projects, taste, time — alongside membership dues.

This is not a spectator program. If you're looking to passively rent a desk, there are plenty of places that sell that. If you're looking to help shape what culture and technology can be in the same room, keep reading.
